01
Foundation
Pick Your Profitable Topic

Before you build anything, you need to confirm that people actually want what you're about to create. This isn't about passion — it's about demand validation.

Most beginners skip this step. They spend weeks creating a product nobody asked for, then wonder why nobody buys it. We're not doing that.

The Quick Validation Trick:

  • Open Google and start typing your topic. Look at autocomplete suggestions — those are real searches from real people.
  • Check Pinterest search bar. Type your niche and see what Pinterest suggests. If Pinterest has suggestions, people are actively searching.
  • Look at "People Also Ask" on Google. These are questions people are literally typing right now.
  • Browse Reddit threads in your niche. What problems do people keep posting about? That's your product idea.

The validation rule: If you can find 3+ places where people are already asking about your topic, there's demand. If you can't find anyone asking — pick a different topic.

✅ Day 1 Action Items
  • →Pick 3 potential topics
  • →Validate each using Google autocomplete + Pinterest search + Reddit
  • →Choose the one with the most visible demand
  • →Write one sentence: "This product helps [WHO] solve [WHAT PROBLEM]"

02
Creation
Create Your Digital Product

Your first product doesn't need to be a masterpiece. It needs to be useful, specific, and finished. Perfectionism kills more businesses than bad ideas do.

What to create:

  • PDF Guide — solve one specific problem in 5-15 pages
  • Checklist — a step-by-step action list people can follow
  • Template Pack — done-for-you templates they can customize
  • Toolkit — a curated list of tools + how to use them

How to build it fast:

  • Use ChatGPT or Claude to draft the content outline
  • Use Canva free tier to design it professionally
  • Use your own experience to add personality and real examples

The AI writes the structure. You add the soul. That combination creates something that looks professional but sounds human.

✅ Day 2 Action Items
  • →Choose your product format (PDF, checklist, template, or toolkit)
  • →Use AI to draft the content outline
  • →Design it in Canva — use a professional template
  • →Edit for personality — add your voice, your examples, your opinions
  • →Export as PDF
