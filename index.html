01
Foundation
Pick Your Profitable Topic

Before you build anything, confirm that people actually want what you're about to create. This isn't about passion — it's about demand validation.

The Quick Validation Trick:

  • Open Google and look at autocomplete suggestions
  • Check Pinterest search bar for your niche
  • Look at "People Also Ask" on Google
  • Browse Reddit threads for common problems

The rule: If you can find 3+ places where people are asking about your topic, there's demand.

✅ Day 1 Action Items
  • →Pick 3 potential topics
  • →Validate each using Google + Pinterest + Reddit
  • →Choose the one with the most demand
  • →Write: "This product helps [WHO] solve [WHAT]"
